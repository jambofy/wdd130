History

Welcome to Jambofy Rafting, where adventure meets nature! Founded in 2024 by passionate river enthusiasts, we are dedicated to providing unforgettable rafting experiences on some of the most stunning rivers. Our mission is to offer safe, exciting, and eco-friendly adventures for all skill levels. Safety is our top priority at Jambofy Rafting. Our highly trained guides and top-notch equipment ensure a secure and thrilling journey for every participant. Whether you're looking for a gentle float or the challenge of navigating through intense rapids, we have something for every adventurer. We are committed to preserving the natural beauty of our rivers and practice sustainable tourism to minimize our environmental impact. By supporting local conservation efforts, we ensure that our rivers remain pristine for future generations. Join us at Jambofy Rafting for the adventure of a lifetime. Create lasting memories, connect with nature, and experience the thrill of riding the rapids. Let's make your rafting dreams come true together!
